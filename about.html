auto_storiesOur Story

From a Single Reactor Line to Global Supply

Stackly began as a small formulation unit focused on solving one problem at a time. Over the years, steady reinvestment in equipment, testing labs and trained personnel turned that unit into a full-scale manufacturing operation.

Today our plants run around the clock to serve partners across more than 40 countries, without losing the batch-level attention we started with.
